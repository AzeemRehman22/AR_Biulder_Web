AR Builders

With over 30 years in the local building industry, and a wide ranging skill-set, AR Builders Kernow have undertaken work for many commercial, insurance and private clients, and along the way have established a solid local reputation for quality, reliability and professionalism. My commercial clients including John Lewis, Argos, Moss Chemists, Chemist, HMV, Oxfam, British Heart Foundation, Focus stores and the Britannia Building Society. I was born in Redruth, lived in Truro and have been involved in the local construction industry for over 25 years. AR Builders Kernow offer a full range of services including footings to drainage, extensions, paving, full aspects of roofing, carpentry first and second fix, rendering and plastering, plumbing, kitchen fitting, subsidence work, flood and fire damage as well as general maintenance. The roofing service that I offer is either flat roof or pitch roof (slate or tile). The flat roof either felt (10 year guarantee) or fibre-glass (25 year guarantee) I offer an honest and friendly service to all my customers, check out my Testimonials Page to see a selection of references received by AR Builders Kernow. But please also check the various photo galleries on the site to see my standard of work, which i class is of a high quality. Please also feel free to view my Facebook Page and leave any questions or feedback. I offer Free Estimates, and I look forward to working with you in the near future - Adrian Rowe
